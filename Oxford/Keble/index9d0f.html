The October air over Oxford hangs thick with the weight of expectation as Rae, Aubrey, Khalid and Merric cross the quieted streets toward Keble College. From the outside, it looks almost like a late-night student party, one of those secret concerts pulled together by word of mouth. However, this isn’t that at all. This is bait. Liam is framed by yellow lamplight, bass in hand, his eyes half-closed in concentration, but scanning the audience with a predator's attention between notes. Mehdi is at the periphery, leaning against a column, speaking in low tones to someone who looks like a student usher. The atmosphere here is charged. The laughter and chatter of mortals seem almost muffled by the heavy, invisible pressure that the vampires recognise only too well. A predator’s presence. Something in the college is awake, watching and waiting. The group halts just outside the open gate to the quad.
