Althir System, Mid Rim, 3976 BBY

Althir III hangs like a green jewel against the darkness. A temperate world of sweeping savannas, fertile valleys and wide, sapphire seas. For centuries Althir has remained quiet on the galactic stage. Too small to be strategic, too distant to be wealthy and too proud to be forgotten. Its people live in a mix of small industrial townships, agricultural communes and the ancient desert enclaves of the native Althiri. However, lately, Althir III has changed. The Republic has established a forward logistics presence here. Nothing official, but enough military advisers, survey teams and supply depots have arrived that the locals whisper of coming war. Rumours spread of Mandalorian raiders in the Outer Rim. A few freighter captains claim to have seen Basilisk war droids in deep space. Others insist that the Mandalorians have returned to their old ways, testing the Republic's borders for weakness. Of course, the Republic denies everything, but fear moves faster than Senate declarations. Today, the air over Althir III is warm and clear, the markets of Vathor’s Landing bustle with traders and travellers, a convoy of ore shipments rolls toward the starport and a Republic survey shuttle rises from the cliffs, its engines humming. Above it all, the sky is calm. (edited)
