      With regard to Thurston’s comments, Dorno replies “ok shiny man.”  “That’s good.”  He seems a bit more at ease.

      “Oh, yeah, I can wake him up.”  Dorno turns to see the eye wound. “Oh my, that’s bad.  One minute.”  Dorno pulls out a familiar salve and runs a generous portion on the caster’s head wound.  The firbolg makes disgusted faces at the eye wound and gets close to smell it.  He thinks for a second and then pulls out a piece of char cloth from his kit.  Still thinking, he keeps the wooden container of the salve handy, and pulls out his tinder kit.

      “Shiny man, hold him down, if you please.  Cover the mouth, too, if you please.”

      “He's going to be upset.”

      Dorno lights the char cloth on fire and lets it smolder for a second, putting it on the ground. He bites his protruding tough once again, and looks at Thurston to indicate that he’s ready.  The firbolg puts his large hand on the man’s forehead and the other on the bolt.  Suddenly and swiftly, Dorno executes three things in a matter of seconds.  He yanks the bolt from the eye socket, stuffs a finger into his salve and puts a large glob of it in the eye socket by inserting half of his large finger, and then shoves the smoldering cloth over the eye, holding it down with his palm as the man, presumably, screams.  Dorno makes faces and wiggles his nose in response to the smell of it, and overall certainly looks as if he is not enjoying this particular healing ritual.

      After holding the cloth down for about thirty seconds, charring his own hand a bit, Dorno removes his palm and shakes away the pain.  "There you go creepy magic man!"  He slaps the man on the shoulder as he rises up a bit.  He repeatedly slaps the man’s hand away any time he reaches for his eye.  “No” “No”  “No!” “Don’t touch, stupid!...please”
