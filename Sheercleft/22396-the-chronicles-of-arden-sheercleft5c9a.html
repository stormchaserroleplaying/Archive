Consent to act here would serve as terms of employment though, allowing her to act by Jex's wishes. Besides, the organisation may not survive a gnoll invasion, another condition of breaking neutrality, though usually employed in cases of invasion by a holy order against their services. 

      This time as she crosses the room to the darkened alcove, she slips her fingers into a recess, and disappears into the shadows.       In the next days Sheercleft erupts in a frenzy of activity. 

      After the news of an marching army to conquer them the sturdy miners where up to the task. There were concerned faces and some worrying whispers of course, but those people had regained there freedom not long ago and weren't going to allow anyone to came and conquer them once again. And this time the had the Archanost with them. 

      Thurston seemed to be everywhere. He sent five members of the naked with with BrÃ¼ndir as his personal guard to Khaz a Grungron but the rest of it aided him preparing the city defenses. 

      "Probably you should go to this mission... clothed " said Aiden at Thurston's side while they were talking to the volunteers. 

      Thurston organized the people into different teams. Some worked on reinforcing the walls and external defenses of the city. Young women and men were put on firefighting duties, at least three barrels were appointed near the most relevant buildings and at the corners of the streets. Buckets were distributed and temas were formed. In case that they tried to start a fire they will be ready to fight it promptly. Also additional barrels were stationed to wet the ceilings and walls of the buildings once the army were at their gates, so the fires will not ignite easily. 

      The great norscan spent a great deal of time at the forge, making weapons and shields for the citizens of Sheercleft. Many had weapons already, thanks to Thor, Tyr and Odin, but many were needed and he lost the count of how many arrowheads he made. 

      The mornings he spent training the ones that would fight for the city. Trying to make them an efficient unit. The rest of the members of the Naked Guard and the seasoned veterans that were already members of the city guard helped him with that. 

      To everyone that he gave a duty he repeated the same. " Don't **** it up. The lives of your family and friends, of all of us, is in your hands.... "

      One night he was at the inn with a tankard of mead and ending a frugal dinner with Aiden and Val he nearly chocked.

      "By Thor's beard!! I almost kill everyone in this town. How I can be so stupid? We must organize guard duties, specially in the wheels and our food resources. We cannot afford a sabotage.       Next morning orders were given to cover such topics.

      Thurston were tired but he worked hard to make sure everyone was involved and had a task to do.
